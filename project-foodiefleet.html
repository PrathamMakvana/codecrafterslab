Project Overview

ResumeCraft Pro is an innovative resume-building solution developed in 2023, built on industry-leading career expertise. We bridge the gap between job seekers and employment opportunities by delivering professionally-designed, ATS-optimized resumes to users across all industries and career levels.

Born from a deep understanding of recruitment processes, we combine professional resume writing principles with modern technology to ensure error-free formatting, impactful content, and interview-winning results.

Our proprietary AI-powered analysis—from skills assessment to job matching—sets us apart, enabling us to offer personalized, data-driven career documents. Committed to accessibility and results, ResumeCraft Pro is transforming job searching by prioritizing user-friendly design, recruiter-approved templates, and real-time performance feedback.

Client Challenge

Job seekers struggle with creating professional resumes that stand out, facing challenges with ATS compatibility issues and ineffective content presentation that leads to missed opportunities and prolonged job searches.

Technologies Used

React Node.js MongoDB Express.js OpenAI API
